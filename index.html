The problem

The hidden operational risk of unmanaged documents

🗄️

Inefficient document retrieval

Time spent manually locating contracts, records, or compliance documents accumulates across customer requests, internal reviews, audits, and reporting cycles — reducing operational efficiency and increasing administrative overhead.

⚠️

Regulatory exposure within paper-based systems

When certificates, policies, and renewal dates are stored in unstructured physical archives, critical deadlines can be overlooked. Missed expiries or incomplete documentation can lead to operational disruption, financial penalties, or regulatory scrutiny.

📦

Escalating document backlog

Ongoing accumulation of forms, invoices, reports, and correspondence compounds over time. Without a structured digitisation strategy, document backlogs expand, making retrieval increasingly complex and resource-intensive.

+
Verified fields
Property Address EPC Rating (A–G) Energy Score Assessor Name Accreditation No. Assessment Date Valid Until
Why it matters: Properties below Band E cannot be legally let. Missing EPCs invalidate Section 21 notices.

+
Verified fields
Property Address Tenant Name(s) Deposit Amount Scheme Name Certificate / Ref No. Date Protected Prescribed Info Served
Why it matters: Deposits must be protected within 30 days. Failure means you cannot serve a Section 21 notice and may face a penalty of up to 3x the deposit.

Verified fields
Property Address Tenant Name Document Type(s) Reference Numbers Date of Check Follow-up Required Follow-up Due Date Checked By

+
Verified fields
